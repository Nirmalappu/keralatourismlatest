Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, and historic landmarks. Visit the iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple, explore the Napier Museum, and relax at Kovalam Beach. Thiruvananthapuram offers a blend of tradition and modernity, making it a must-visit destination in Kerala.
